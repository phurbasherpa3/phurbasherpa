About Me

Connecting Two Worlds

I was born in Lukla, the historic gateway to Mount Everest. Growing up in the shadow of the world's tallest peaks, adventure is built into my DNA. Over the years, this environment inspired a dual drive within me: to guide others up these giants, and to document these extreme human journeys on screen.

Today, I serve as the Chief Operating Officer (COO) at Khumbu Excursion Pvt. Ltd., where I manage operations and lead mountaineers, trekkers, and climbers across Nepal. We make high-altitude trekking safe, immersive, and unforgettable for adventurers from all walks of life.

Simultaneously, I run Phurba Film Production. Handling the entire creative pipeline—from scriptwriting and preproduction through direction, cinematography, and final edits. I specialize in crafting raw, emotional, and visually arresting commercial stories and documentaries that help brands truly shine.
